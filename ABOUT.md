# About DEEPLBMNet

## Introduction

DEEPLBMNet is an independent research initiative dedicated to advancing Physics-Inspired Artificial Intelligence through novel deep learning architectures based on the Lattice Boltzmann Method (LBM).

The project explores how principles from computational physics can inspire more efficient, interpretable, and scalable artificial intelligence models for a wide range of scientific and engineering applications.

---

## Mission

Our mission is to bridge computational physics and modern AI by developing innovative architectures that combine physical modeling with deep learning to address real-world challenges.

---

## Vision

We envision a future where physics-inspired neural networks become a major paradigm for scientific machine learning, enabling more robust, explainable, and computationally efficient AI systems.

---

## Research Areas

Current and future research includes:

* Physics-Inspired Artificial Intelligence
* Lattice Boltzmann Method
* Scientific Machine Learning
* Medical Imaging
* Computer Vision
* Image Restoration
* Image Inpainting
* Image Segmentation
* Image Super-Resolution
* Object Detection
* Natural Language Processing
* Time Series Forecasting
* Cybersecurity
* Scientific Computing
* Robotics

DEEPLBMNet supports open science and reproducible research. Source code, pretrained models, and tutorials will be released progressively following the publication or acceptance of the corresponding scientific work.

# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to fostering a welcoming, respectful, and collaborative environment for everyone participating in the DEEPLBMNet project, regardless of background, experience, or affiliation.

Our goal is to promote open scientific collaboration, constructive discussion, and responsible innovation in Physics-Inspired Artificial Intelligence.

---

## Expected Behavior

All participants are expected to:

* Be respectful and professional.
* Welcome diverse perspectives and ideas.
* Provide constructive feedback.
* Support open and collaborative scientific research.
* Respect intellectual property and academic integrity.
* Communicate clearly and courteously.
* Help create a positive and inclusive community.

---

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors are not acceptable:

* Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.
* Offensive or abusive language.
* Trolling or intentionally disruptive behavior.
* Publishing confidential or proprietary information without permission.
* Plagiarism or academic misconduct.
* Any activity that violates applicable laws or ethical standards.

---

## Research Ethics

DEEPLBMNet encourages responsible research practices, including:

* Scientific honesty and transparency.
* Proper citation of previous work.
* Reproducible research whenever possible.
* Respect for datasets, licenses, and intellectual property.
* Ethical use of artificial intelligence technologies.

---

## Enforcement

Project maintainers reserve the right to remove comments, discussions, issues, pull requests, or other contributions that violate this Code of Conduct.

Repeated or serious violations may result in temporary or permanent exclusion from participation in the project.

---

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces, including:

* GitHub repositories
* Discussions
* Issues
* Pull Requests
* Documentation
* Official communication channels

---

## Contact

If you experience or witness behavior that violates this Code of Conduct, please contact the project maintainers through the official website:

**https://deeplbmnet.com**

---

## Acknowledgment

DEEPLBMNet is committed to building a professional, respectful, and collaborative research community that advances Physics-Inspired Artificial Intelligence for the benefit of science and society.

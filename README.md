# About

**DEEPLBMNet** is an open scientific research initiative dedicated to advancing the next generation of **Physics-Inspired Artificial Intelligence (PIAI)** by integrating the mathematical principles of the **Lattice Boltzmann Method (LBM)** with modern deep learning.

The project explores innovative neural architectures inspired by physical processes to develop efficient, interpretable, and scientifically grounded artificial intelligence models for scientific computing and real-world applications.

DEEPLBMNet aims to bridge computational physics, applied mathematics, and artificial intelligence while promoting reproducible research, open collaboration, and high-quality educational resources.

---

# Vision

Our vision is to establish DEEPLBMNet as a leading research platform for Physics-Inspired Artificial Intelligence, contributing to the development of robust, explainable, and high-performance AI systems inspired by fundamental physical principles.

# Why DEEPLBMNet?

DEEPLBMNet investigates a new direction in artificial intelligence by incorporating concepts from computational physics into neural network design. Rather than relying exclusively on conventional architectures, the project explores physics-inspired approaches that aim to improve computational efficiency, interpretability, and robustness across a wide range of AI applications.

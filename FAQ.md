# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

## What is DEEPLBMNet?

DEEPLBMNet is an independent research initiative developing Physics-Inspired Artificial Intelligence architectures based on the Lattice Boltzmann Method (LBM).

---

## What makes DEEPLBMNet different?

Unlike conventional deep learning architectures, DEEPLBMNet explores computational physics principles to design more efficient, interpretable, and scalable AI models.

---

## Is DEEPLBMNet open source?

The project will become progressively open source. Source code will be released after the corresponding scientific work has been published or officially accepted.

---

## Which research areas are covered?

DEEPLBMNet explores applications in:

* Medical Imaging
* Computer Vision
* Scientific Machine Learning
* Image Restoration
* Image Segmentation
* Image Inpainting
* Object Detection
* Robotics
* Cybersecurity
* Natural Language Processing
* Time Series Forecasting
* Scientific Computing
